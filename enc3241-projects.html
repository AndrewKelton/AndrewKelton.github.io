Digital Portfolio Progress

 My portfolio is coming along quite well. Although I have already made this previously, this assignment has allowed me to expand and revise it. While revising and creating my portfolio, I have enhanced my skills in HTML, since that is what I used to make this. Specifically, I have learned a lot about making websites visibly appealing from different media types (PC, iPhone, etc.). I have also learned about servers and deployments of updates.

 I think my page layouts are quite good and are easy to navigate. However, I may change the color scheme to a simple white and black scheme instead. One aspect that is difficult to grasp for me is the design of a website. Even though I consider myself a good programmer and computer scientst, websites are an entirely different aspect of programming. I am not the most artistic person and it can be quite difficult to imagine a certain design implementation. To help me understand the design aspect of things, I have been using resources provided in the textbook and W3schools's website to learn different HTML tasks. One question I have is, what color scheme is best for a portfolio? In my opinion, portfolios are supposed to be designed in a way the author thinks is appealing and shows their skills and creativity. However, I am unsure if my color scheme is too dark or looks 'cheesy'. Overall, I think my portfolio is being put together quite well and is pretty decent for being entirely written in HTML having little prior experience.
